rsync [source dir] [destination dir] (can use [username]@[ip]:[path/to/destination] for any of them)
    -r (recursive)
    -a (recursive & archive)
    -n (double-check)
    -v (verbose)
    -anv
    -anz
    (use this for using ssh key [-e "ssh [-p port]  -i [path/to/key]"] before [username]@[ip] . . .)
